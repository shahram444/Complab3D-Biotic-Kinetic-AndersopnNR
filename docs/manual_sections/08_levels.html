LEVEL 1: FIRST BREATH
Chapter 1 — The Soil Frontier
GOAL3 colonies
MAP30×20
RIVALS0
TOXICNone
Strategy: Pure tutorial level. No enemies, no hazards. Wide open pore space with 70% porosity. O2, NO3, and CH4 available. Learn the basics: WASD/Arrow movement, eating on contact, pressing SPACE to divide.
